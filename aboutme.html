About Me

THE BEGINNING

More you learn, the more you know, and the further you can go.

As a kid, I always played video games, so naturally when I finished high school, I turned to this field. So, I did a year of 3D. Then, I learned that later on, I would have to work for studios that would tell me what to do, but I didn't want to work like a robot, making 3D chairs and tables endlessly... Well, that's just my luck! (PS: I still love playing them, lol).

CURRENTLY


Hi! I'm Camille Declercq, I'm 20 yo and I want to become an artistic director.

I am currently studying at ESD Lyon as a Digital Project Manager. In parallel, I have co-founded a clothing brand with my friend Tom, which you can find here.
This project is particularly dear to me as it has been a dream since I was a child, and we are mindful of the environment and our planet by using recycled fabrics.

This brand has taught me to be organized and has allowed me to develop my skills. Ultimately, I would like to open a pop-up store and perhaps make a living from it. However, I would also like to have my own company and do rebranding for clients.

I would like to be able to settle in the south or in an English-speaking country to discover new cultures that can inspire me in my work.
